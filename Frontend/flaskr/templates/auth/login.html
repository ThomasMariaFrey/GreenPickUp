Green is smart!

We aim to deliver parcels using mobile pick-up stations. Customers will be able to pick up their orders at the places that they already frequent anayways. This will reduce traffic flow and emission as the pick-up stations will move with the populace.

What is this?

The Green parcel delivery project is about delivering a smart alternative to classic pick up stations and home delivery of parcels.
To learn more about the green parcel delivery project click the button below.



Sustainability

by reducing the carbon emissions caused by delivery vehicles

Decentralized Pick-Up System

making detours no longer necessary to pick up ones parcel

Zero-Emission Delivery

through the usage of electric bicycles for the movement of the mobile pick up stations
